SAMSUNG GALAXY A52S

The Samsung Galaxy A52s will be the first phone to launch in September. The device is already available in global markets and will be available now. The Galaxy A52s is powered by Snapdragon 778G with up to 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage. In terms of visuals, you get a 6.5-inch Full HD+ Super AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate.



Some More Special Updates

Further, in the camera department, the Galaxy A52s sports a quad-camera setup with 64MP wide, a 12MP ultrawide, a 5MP macro, and a 5MP depth sensor. There is a 32MP shooter for selfies and video calls. The device packs in a 4,500mAh-capacity battery, 25W fast charging, and IP67 water resistance rating.

REDMI 10 PRIME

After unveiling five Redmi Note 10 series devices, Xiaomi India will launch the first number series device under the Redmi 10 lineup on September 3. The Redmi 10 Prime could be a re-branded version of the Redmi 10 which was unveiled globally recently.



Some More Special Updates

The Redmi 10 Prime will come with a new MediaTek Helio G88 processor. It could pack in a 6.5-inch FHD+ 90Hz display along with a 5,000mAh battery, 18W fast charging. In terms of optics, the Redmi 10 comes with a 50MP main camera and an 8MP selfie shooter on the front.

MOTO EDGE 20 PRO

In August, the Moto Edge 20 and Moto Edge 20 Fusion devices were unveiled. In September, the Moto Edge 20 Pro is expected to arrive in India. The Moto Edge 20 Pro is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 870 and sports a 6.7-inch Full HD+ 10-bit OLED with a 144Hz screen refresh rate.



Some More Special Updates

In terms of optics, the Moto Edge 20 Pro sports a 108MP main camera, an 8MP periscope camera with 5x optical and up to 50x digital zoom, and a 16MP ultra-wide angle shooter. The selfies are handled by a 32MP shooter on the front. Lastly, the Moto Edge 20 Pro comes with a 4,500mAh battery coupled with a 30W fast charger.
